Shymbulak mountain resort

About resort

Favorable natural conditions attract tourists, climbers, and skiers to this area with accessible terrain, and make it possible to lay ski slopes with a vertical drop of 900-1000 m. In this regard, since 1954, the Chimbulak ski sports base began to operate here. The length of the track for sports descent from the Talgar Pass is 3,500 m, giant slalom 1,500 m, average width 25 m, slope 11–29 °. Such parameters allow athletes to develop maximum speeds along its entire length.
