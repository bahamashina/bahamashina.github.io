Big Almaty Lake

About Lake

The hollow of the lake is of tectonic origin, of complex shape, with a general bias to the north. The length of the lake is 1.6 km, the width is from 0.75 to 1 km.The length of the coastline is 3 km, the depth is 30-40 m, the area of ​​the mirror is 0.9 square kilometers, the volume of the water mass is 14 cubic kilometers.The maximum water level in August, the minimum in February. The level fluctuations reach 20 m. The shores of the lake are steep and steep. The Bolshaya Almatinka River flows into the lake from the south.It is part of the Ile-Alatau National Park.
