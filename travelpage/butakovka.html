Butakovka

About Waterfall

Butakovsky Falls is the largest waterfall located near Almaty. There are two waterfalls in the Butakovsky Gorge - Nizhny (Bolshoi) and Upper. Ascent to the waterfall begins at an altitude of 1787 m above sea level. After walking along a dirt road among beautiful fir trees, we approach the Extreme base. Having passed through its territory, we see a pointer on the left and go down to the spring. The waterfall is formed by a small stream, which falls almost vertically down along the cliff.
